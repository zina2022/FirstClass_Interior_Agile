Restaurants

SoHo House Auckland

We did a lot of research into the American diner experience, taking inspiration from the finishes used within the restaurants. We have kept the materials true to those used back in 1950s and incorporating those into our design.

The Harvey House Auckland

'Captivate Interiors' primary design goal for The Harvey House was to create a warm and inviting restaurant that marries the familiar comfort of a Wisconsin supper club with a uniquely transportive experience evocative of the golden age of train travel.

Lady Bund in Auckland

Both Asian and European elements have influenced the Asian Fusion interior and the gastronomic concept. Beyond culture, Captivate Interiors has taken up the challenge to create a space with a strong identity that ought to be as vibrant and embezzling in two very distinctive contexts: lively and cozy restaurant in the daytime, intimate and fancy dining lounge during night.

Hotels

Four Seasons Auckland

This Four Seasons has made the Neoclassical landmark so lovely that it feels much more like a palace than an old jail. The soaring ceilings and giant wood-frame windows help you to enjoy some of the perks that perhaps even the prisoners once had: views of the Skytower and the Auckland Harbour.

Triple Creek Ranch

The 1- to 3-bedroom log cabins have glowing cedar interiors, stone fireplaces, and classic and contemporary Western art, ranging from Frederic Remington to Oleg Stavrowsky. You can live a lot of experiences while your stay from fly-fishing and horseback riding to panning for sapphires.

Grand Solmar Land’s End Resort & Spa

Details such as the inlaid walkways and stone archways in the public spaces, and the room’s hand carved wooden furniture, elegant ironwork, and pops of red make the contemporary buildings stand out.

Offices

TikTok Office in Auckland

Captivate Interiors vibrant design of the company's first New Zealand headquarters is housed in a building in the Newmarket sector of Auckland. TikTok comes to New Zealand on three floors that incorporate two additional mezzanines to total 120,000 square feet. Naturally, a sense of movement, intrinsic to TikTok’s culture, pervades.

Annal Headquarters in Auckland

A high-performance HQ that reflects its evolving identity as a premier, luxury lifestyle brand. The workspace is designed to increase creativity and innovation by bringing people together—featuring open floor plans and interconnected levels with comfortable corners carved out for private conversations and relaxing breaks.

The Cubes

One of the most impressive new co-working spaces of 2018, this 1,200-square-metre space in Auckland is defined by a gridded layout, which caters to a variety working styles. Furnishings are all in pastel colours, with sage-green sofas teamed with pink and beige cushions, and matching tables and chairs.

Homes

House in the Shore

In a North Shore house devised by architecture firm Marmol Radziner with interior design by Captivate Interiors, the great room is furnished with midcentury-modern staples like Mies van der Rohe’s Barcelona chairs and ottoman by Knoll.

Loft in the City

In the Auckland City loft of architect Steven Harris and interior designer Reinhard Lohengramm, monumental bookshelves delineate the living and dining areas, which feature a 1960 Georges Braque print (at left) and Ib Kofod-Larsen chairs at the Rees Roberts + Partners dining table.

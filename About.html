CEO:

Olajide Olayinka Williams Olatunji:

At just the tender age of 21 (currently 28), Olajide 'JJ' Olatunji founded Captivate Interiors as a response to the current interior design scene in Auckland. 'Ay you know fam, a lot of the interior design that I've seen within restaurants, hotels, offices- bruv it's all dead fam. I'm here to change that'.

Over the course of 7 years, he has accomplished just that, being responsible for the interior design of world-renowned landmarks located in Auckland thanks to his vision. 'Ay dunno mans out here changing the interior design game innit HAHAHA!'

'I'm just glad I got to have an impact on the world through my passion and letting my creativity be displayed for the world to see. However, I couldn't have done it without my amazing staff.'


Lead Designer:

Kanye West

'Legend' would be an understatement to describe this figure. Kanye West, designer, rapper, philanthropist, icon. Kanye, after releasing Donda, his latest album out at the time of writing, disappeared from the face of the planet. Having seemingly retired from rapping indefinitely, he decided to venture into the interior design world as to flex another skill of his varied repertoire. "God lead me here. My mother lead me here. It's nice and relaxing doing this and allows me to show everyone what I'm all about".






Interior Designer:

Ted Nivison

Ted Nivison has a burning passion for interior design and is also responsible for helping furnish most offices and homes. Citing back to his memory of the milkman. "The milkman insulted my precious home when he took a glance behind me. It's something that stuck with me to this day and I use it as a motivation to better everyone's interior."







Interior Designer:

Trent

After spending time as a professional footballer and succeeding well, he was struck with a severe injury that would impact his career forever, paralysing him from the waist down.

"I was deeply saddened after the injury, I didn't know what to do from that point on... but after spending some good time self-evaluating wondering what I'm going to do next, I decided to pick up my real passion, Interior Design, and I have never looked back since."





Interior Designer:

Ronnie Coleman

"I love interior design!" He shouted as he strutted his stuff at Mr Olympia, a prestigious bodybuilding competition. After winning for the 250th time in a row, he decided to retire a champion and made an impact on the interior design world instead. "I take care of my body like it's a temple. So why not take care on an actual temple instead? Or a mansion? Castle? You know? That's my mindset and I'm glad I picked up on this passion of mine because now I can't stop."
